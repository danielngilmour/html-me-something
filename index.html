Thanksgiving in India

As a programmer in LaunchCode 101 NWA, I am learning to use html, css, and javascript to share experiences via the web. Today I am sharing the story of the last big trip I got to take before covid-19 shut down the world.

  • Origin: Bentonville, Arkansas
  • Destination: Kerala, India
  • Reason: Why not? Also... our friend needed us to bring a can of pumpkin for the pie.
I love to travel whenever I just can't take the sheer comfort of life in the middle of the USA. When my brother proposed a visit to Kerala, India to visit a friend for Thanksgiving, I was excited to go, but did not realize just how rare an opportunity the trip would be after international travel came to an abrupt halt in 2020 due to covid-19.
